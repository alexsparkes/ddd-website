Frequently Asked Questions

Is UniPets free to use?
Yes! The basic version of UniPets is completely free. We also offer a Premium version with additional pet types and features for a small monthly fee.
Which devices support UniPets?
UniPets is available on Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android devices, so you can take your pet with you anywhere!
What happens if I forget to feed my pet?
Don't worry! Your pet won't die, but they will become unhappy and may refuse to help with your study reminders until you care for them again.
How do I connect with other UniPets users?
Through our in-app community feature! You can join groups based on your university, course, or interests, and arrange meetups with fellow pet owners.
